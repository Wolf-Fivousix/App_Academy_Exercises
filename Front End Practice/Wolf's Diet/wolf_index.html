Backstory

This is the prime source of energy for predatorial creatures like the Wolf. Please keep your hands away from the creature while feeding. As that could be seen as an atempt of stealing it's meal.

Ingredients

    Acquire these in order:
  • Prey
  • Plenty of Space
  • Sharp Fangs and Powerful Muscles
  • Sharp Tool
  • Fire Source
  • Salt

Methods / Directions

  1. Locate an appropriate Prey for the meal. Usually found in plains. It can range from a small animal like a Rabbit, to a large creature like a Deer. Use your own hunger and pack size as an indicator of how much you will need to fufill your starving stomach. Remember: No waste!
  2. Make sure to leave Plenty of Space for your prey to run for it's life. A good hunt makes the meal more enjoyable.
  3. Remember to keep your Fangs Sharp and your Muscles ready for the kill. Your Prey will NOT give up before it's dead. YOU need to ensure that you're the one putting your game out of The Game. DO NOT release your Prey until it's definitely dead. A dieing animal can have surprise strength and fatally injure you.
  4. A Sharp Tool for slicing the deceased Prey quickly as possible. One like those humans use should sufice. Make sure to compensate for the lack of oppositable thumbs. Hand shard objects with care.
  5. Secure a Fire Source. Again, one of the humans should be able to provide an adequate option. The equipments called "grill" are most suitable, but an open fire can be used with minor adaptations.
  6. Salt is an essential nutrient and will enhance the flavor of your meal. Again, can easly be acquired from the bipedal creatures called "humans".
